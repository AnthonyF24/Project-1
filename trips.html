The Trip

Our luxury fishing boat charters offer an unforgettable 5-10 day journey, starting from the historic port of Kinsale and traveling clockwise along Ireland’s stunning coastline. Each day brings new adventures as we explore some of Ireland's most iconic landmarks and pristine waters. Depending on the duration of your trip, we'll moor overnight at renowned harbors such as Baltimore, Dingle, and Galway, where you can enjoy local culture and cuisine. As we continue northward, you’ll experience the rugged beauty of the Wild Atlantic Way, including stops near the Cliffs of Moher and the mystical Skellig Michael. For longer charters, your journey may culminate at Malin Head, Ireland’s northernmost point, with a luxurious final night at a 5-star hotel—providing the perfect way to cap off your adventure. With tailored itineraries that allow for both thrilling fishing and leisurely coastal exploration, this charter offers the ultimate blend of relaxation and excitement, delivering a unique way to discover Ireland's coastline.

The Boat

The Viking Yachts 82 is a luxurious and powerful sportfishing yacht, perfect for fishing charters. Measuring 82 feet in length, it combines high-performance capabilities with top-tier comfort. Equipped with advanced fishing amenities, such as a spacious cockpit, state-of-the-art navigation systems, and tournament-grade fishing gear, this vessel ensures a productive and enjoyable fishing experience. The Viking 82 also features elegant interiors with ample seating, modern conveniences, and luxurious accommodations, making it ideal for both avid anglers and those looking to relax on the water in style.

Accommodation

The Viking Yachts 82 offers exceptional guest accommodations, designed to provide comfort and luxury for up to 12 guests. It features five beautifully appointed staterooms, each with en-suite bathrooms, providing a private and relaxing retreat. The master suite is particularly spacious, with a king-sized bed, ample storage, and elegant finishes. Additional guest staterooms are equally refined, with a mix of queen and twin beds, all designed for a restful stay. With modern amenities, climate control, and stylish interiors, the Viking 82 ensures that guests enjoy a comfortable and first-class experience during their time on board.

Catering

Onboard the Viking Yachts 82, guests can indulge in exceptional catering, featuring the finest Irish cuisine with a blend of land and seafood dishes. The menu highlights fresh Atlantic seafood, including oysters, lobster, and crab, alongside traditional Irish favorites like lamb stew, beef fillets, and colcannon. Expert chefs craft each meal using locally sourced ingredients, ensuring a gourmet dining experience. In addition to formal meals, guests have access to snacks 24/7, allowing them to enjoy light bites or refreshments at any time, making their stay on the water as comfortable and delicious as possible.

Fishing

Fishing charters in Ireland offer an unforgettable experience, with access to some of the best fishing waters in Europe. Anglers can target a wide variety of fish, including cod, pollock, mackerel, and sea bass along the stunning coastline. For those seeking a thrilling challenge, Ireland is also renowned for its bluefin tuna, offering the opportunity to participate in multi-day catch and release tuna fishing sessions. These sustainable adventures allow anglers to reel in these powerful giants, experiencing their impressive strength while contributing to conservation efforts, making it an exciting and responsible way to enjoy Ireland’s rich fishing waters.

Explore our Coasts

In addition to world-class fishing, our charters offer the option to take a break and explore Ireland’s breathtaking coastline and famous landmarks. Guests can enjoy scenic tours along the Wild Atlantic Way, stopping at iconic locations like the Cliffs of Moher, Skellig Michael, and the historic Hook Lighthouse. Overnight mooring is available at renowned harbors such as Kinsale, Dingle, and Galway, where you can relax and enjoy local culture. We also offer inland excursions and activities, from golf at championship courses to guided whiskey tours. If you're unsure of what to do, our team is happy to provide personalized recommendations for your adventure.

Snorkelling and Scuba Diving

For those seeking underwater adventure, we offer the option to snorkel and scuba dive in some of Ireland’s most stunning waters, perfect for exploring shipwrecks and vibrant reefs. If qualified, guests can dive down to see historic wrecks like the RMS Lusitania or discover the rich marine life that inhabits Ireland's reefs. The crystal-clear waters around spots like Cork and Donegal provide excellent visibility, allowing divers to immerse themselves in Ireland’s underwater world. Whether you're a seasoned diver or prefer to snorkel closer to the surface, these excursions add a thrilling dimension to your charter experience.

Transfers

For an added touch of luxury, we offer VIP transfers to and from the boat’s berth at an additional cost. Guests can choose to arrive in style via helicopter or a VIP limousine, ensuring a seamless and comfortable journey. Whether you're traveling from the airport, a hotel, or another location of your choice, our transfer service guarantees a stress-free start and end to your charter experience. With personalized pick-up and drop-off options, we make sure every aspect of your trip is smooth and convenient, so you can focus on enjoying your time on the water.
