The Trip

Our luxury fishing charters offer an unforgettable 5-10 day journey along Ireland’s breathtaking coastline, starting from Kinsale. Each day brings new adventures as we explore iconic landmarks and pristine waters. Depending on your trip length, we’ll moor at renowned harbors like Baltimore, Dingle, and Galway, where you can savor local culture and cuisine. Highlights include the Wild Atlantic Way, Cliffs of Moher, and Skellig Michael. Longer trips may culminate at Malin Head, with a final night at a 5-star hotel. Tailored itineraries blend thrilling fishing with leisurely exploration, offering the ultimate coastal experience.

The Boat

The Viking Yachts 82 is a luxurious 82-foot sportfishing yacht, ideal for fishing charters. It offers high-performance fishing capabilities with a spacious cockpit, advanced navigation, and tournament-grade gear. Inside, elegant interiors, modern amenities, and comfortable accommodations make it perfect for both serious anglers and those seeking a stylish, relaxing experience on the water.

Accommodation

The Viking Yachts 82 offers luxurious accommodations for up to 12 guests, featuring five elegant staterooms, each with en-suite bathrooms. The spacious master suite includes a king-sized bed and ample storage, while additional staterooms offer a mix of queen and twin beds for a restful stay. Modern amenities, climate control, and stylish interiors ensure a first-class experience throughout the yacht.

Catering

Onboard the Viking Yachts 82, guests enjoy exceptional catering with the finest Irish cuisine, featuring fresh Atlantic seafood like oysters, lobster, and crab, alongside Irish classics like lamb stew and beef fillets. Expert chefs use locally sourced ingredients for a gourmet dining experience. Snacks are available 24/7, ensuring comfort and delicious meals throughout the stay.

Summary

Our charters offer world-class fishing and scenic tours of Ireland's stunning coastline, visiting landmarks like the Cliffs of Moher, Skellig Michael, and Hook Lighthouse. Guests can moor at harbors such as Kinsale, Dingle, and Galway to experience local culture. Inland activities include championship golf and guided whiskey tours, with personalized recommendations available. Fishing in Ireland’s rich waters offers a variety of fish like cod, pollock, mackerel, and sea bass, with the option for catch-and-release bluefin tuna fishing, combining adventure with conservation.
